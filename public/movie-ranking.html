Dimitra recomends: Movies

My top 3 for now are:

Pride and Prejudice(2005)

A love story, written by the famous Jane Austen, where the center of the story is around the life of Elizabeth Bennet and Mr.Darcy. Sweet Romance, great cinematography and mesmerizing soundtracks are gonna be the core of your experience. Enjoy! (Stars 5/5)

Lord of the Rings: The return of the king (2003)

The last part of the trilogy the Lord of the Rings by Tolkien. We will finally learn if Frodo and Sam made it, we will see epic batles, great music soundtracks. An emotional movie for one of the best trilogies of cinematograph. If you haven't watched it, you must.(Stars 5/5)

Captain Fantastic (2016)

You may think it is about superheroes but it is not. What happens when a couple decides to raise up their family far away from the known civilization and with their own unique way. A movie that will devide you, make you think and inspire you. Or maybe not. Watch this movie in a movie festival or outdoors is a must. P.S. One of the best covers of Sweet Child O' Mine by Guns n' Roses. Stars(5/5)
